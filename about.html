About Golden Apple

Golden Apple Technologies was born in Kampala from the mentorship of CEO Nathan. What started as a coding dream has evolved into a tech empire—empowering youth, building tools like Codemind VOL 23.0, and launching platforms like iAM Scholar.

Our Vision

We aim to build legendary digital experiences that blend creativity, code, and culture. From educational games to cybersecurity tools, our mission is to shape Africa’s tech future.
