Our Story

Golden Apple Technologies was born in Kampala from the mentorship of CEO Nathan. What started as a coding dream has evolved into a tech empire—empowering youth, building tools like Codemind VOL 23.0, and launching platforms like iAM Scholar.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create technology that makes a difference. Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of digital innovation in Africa, developing solutions that solve real-world problems and inspire the next generation of tech leaders.
